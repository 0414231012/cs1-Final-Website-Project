How to build a skateboard

Learn how to build a skateboard from scratch, including selecting the right parts and assembling them for the perfect ride.

I recommend this video How to Build a Skateboard By Tactics


Step 1: Gather Your Parts

You will need the following parts to build your skateboard:

  • Grip Tape
  • Deck
  • Trucks
  • Wheels & Bearings

If you dont these have these parts, Make sure to always support your local skateshop

Step 2: Assemble the Trucks

Attach the trucks to the deck using the provided hardware. Make sure they are securely fastened.

Step 3: Attach the Wheels

Put your bearing in the wheels, Mount the wheels onto the trucks using the axle nuts. Ensure they spin freely.

Step 4: Apply Grip Tape

Peel the backing off the grip tape and carefully apply it to the top of the deck. Use a file or razor blade to trim the excess tape.
Make sure to press hard with your file to ensure a smooth cut when cutting the edges and cut fast.

Step 5: Final Adjustments

Check all components to ensure everything is tight and secure. Adjust the trucks to the tightness you like for better control.

Step 6: Test Ride

Take your newly built skateboard for a test ride in a safe area to get a feel for it and make any necessary adjustments.

Enjoy your ride and keep practicing!
