Tricks

Skateboard tricks from beginner to advanced levels.

1. Ollie

The Ollie is the most important trick in skateboarding. You have to pop the tail of the skateboard off the ground and jump into the air with the board.
Mastering the Ollie is very important for learning more tricks. and you should always practice the ollie whenever you can
The better the Ollie the better the skater.

2. Popshuvit / Shuvit

The Popshuvit or shuvit is a trick where the skateboard spins 180 degrees under you
this trick could be learned without the ollie.

3. Kickflip

The Kickflip is an advanced trick that builds on the ollie. In this trick, you use your front foot to flick the board, causing it to spin along its length while you jump into the air.
This trick takes a lot of practice to master, but it's a great trick for any skateboarder looking to progress their skills.

4. Heelflip

The Heelflip is just like the Kickflip, but instead of using your front foot to flick the board, you use your heel to make it spin in the opposite direction.
This trick also requires a solid ollie and a lot of practice to get the timing and flick just right.
